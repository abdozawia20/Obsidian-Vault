# Vehicle Inspection — Flutter: Functional Document

> **Product**: Asset Rental Platform — Vehicle Variant
> **Domain**: Vehicle Inspection
> **Module**: Customer Mobile App — Inspection Results (Read-Only)

---

## 1. Purpose & Scope

Inspection management is Desk-only. The Flutter app exposes only **exit inspection deposit deductions** for tenant review and dispute.

---

## 2. Screen Requirements

| # | Requirement |
|---|---|
| VF-060 | Tenant can view exit deduction line items (description, amount, photo) on agreement detail screen |
| VF-061 | Entry inspection results are **not** shown — only exit deductions visible |
