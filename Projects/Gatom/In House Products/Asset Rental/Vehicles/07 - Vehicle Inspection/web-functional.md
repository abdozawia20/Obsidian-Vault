# Vehicle Inspection — Web: Functional Document

> **Product**: Asset Rental Platform — Vehicle Variant
> **Domain**: Vehicle Inspection
> **Module**: Customer-Facing Website — Inspection Results (Read-Only)

---

## 1. Purpose & Scope

Inspection management is Desk-only. The portal exposes only **exit inspection deposit deductions** for tenant review and dispute.

---

## 2. Page Requirements

| # | Requirement |
|---|---|
| VW-060 | Tenant can view exit deduction line items (description, amount, photo) on agreement detail in `/my-rentals` |
| VW-061 | Entry inspection results are **not** shown to tenants — only exit deductions visible |
