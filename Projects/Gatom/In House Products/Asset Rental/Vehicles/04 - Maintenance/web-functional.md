# Maintenance — Web: Functional Document

> **Product**: Asset Rental Platform — Vehicle Variant
> **Domain**: Maintenance Scheduling

---

## 1. Purpose & Scope

Maintenance scheduling is entirely Desk-based. The only customer-facing impact is that maintenance windows appear as **blocked dates** on the availability calendar — with no reason disclosed.

---

## 2. Requirements

| # | Requirement |
|---|---|
| VW-013 | Availability calendar includes maintenance windows as blocked dates (no reason shown) |

---

## 3. Business Rules

1. Customers see only opaque unavailability — maintenance, other renters, and holds are all identical "blocked" states.
