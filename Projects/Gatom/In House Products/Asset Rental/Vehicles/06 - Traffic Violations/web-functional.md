# Traffic Violations — Web: Functional Document

> **Product**: Asset Rental Platform — Vehicle Variant
> **Domain**: Traffic Violations

---

## 1. Purpose & Scope

Traffic violation management is primarily Desk-based. The web portal shows the tenant their violation charges in the agreement detail but does **not** provide a self-report form (self-report is app-only).

---

## 2. Page Requirements

> Violation charges appear as line items on the agreement detail in `/my-rentals` (covered by the base accounting portal). No additional web-specific pages are needed for violations.

---

## 3. Security Requirements

| Requirement | Description |
|---|---|
| **Violation evidence** | Documents accessible only to Fleet Manager and Accountant roles |
