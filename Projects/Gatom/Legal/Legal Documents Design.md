## ⚠️ Legal Notice

All documents in this folder are **templates** prepared for operational and planning purposes.

> **Important: Gatom is not a registered legal entity.** It is an unincorporated group of self-employed individuals operating under a shared trading name. This means:
> - Contracts are entered into by **individual members personally**, not a company
> - Individual members may be **personally liable** for obligations under these agreements
> - There is no limited liability protection unless individuals are separately registered (e.g., as sole traders or freelancers in their own jurisdictions)

These documents are intentionally **jurisdiction-neutral** and **internationally framed**. Governing law is agreed between parties at the time of signing. We recommend both parties seek independent legal advice in their own jurisdiction before execution.
