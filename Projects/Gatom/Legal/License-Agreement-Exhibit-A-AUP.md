# EXHIBIT A — ACCEPTABLE USE POLICY (AUP)
**Attached to License Agreement v1.0 | Effective: [DATE]**

---

## PURPOSE

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") defines the permitted and prohibited uses of Gatom's software platforms ("Software"). It applies to all Licensees, authorized users, and any person accessing the Software on Licensee's behalf.

---

## PERMITTED USES

Licensees may use the Software to:

- Manage internal business operations (rental management, client records, scheduling, invoicing);
- Generate reports and analytics for internal decision-making;
- Onboard and manage authorized employees and contractors of Licensee's organization;
- Integrate with approved third-party services as documented by Gatom.

---

## PROHIBITED USES

Licensees shall **not** use the Software to:

### Legal Violations
- Conduct or facilitate any activity that violates any applicable local, national, or international law or regulation, including Egyptian law and, where applicable, EU law;
- Engage in fraudulent, deceptive, or misleading activities;
- Facilitate illegal real estate or vehicle subletting, rental scams, or unauthorized property management;
- Launder money or facilitate financial crimes.

### Platform Abuse
- Attempt to gain unauthorized access to Gatom systems, servers, or other users' accounts;
- Introduce malware, viruses, or any malicious code into the Software or connected systems;
- Perform penetration testing, vulnerability scanning, or load testing on Gatom infrastructure without prior written authorization;
- Circumvent usage limits, seat restrictions, or license controls.

### Data Misuse
- Process or store data that violates the privacy rights of individuals under Egyptian Law 151/2020 or applicable data protection laws;
- Use the platform to process sensitive personal data (health records, financial account details, government IDs) beyond the platform's intended scope without appropriate safeguards;
- Collect or harvest third-party personal data without consent.

### IP & Content
- Upload, distribute, or display content that is defamatory, obscene, harassing, or infringes any intellectual property rights;
- Scrape, aggregate, or extract Gatom's platform data or features for competitive analysis.

---

## ENFORCEMENT

Gatom reserves the right to:

- Suspend Licensee access immediately upon confirmed material violation of this AUP;
- Investigate suspected violations, including reviewing logs and access records;
- Report illegal activity to relevant law enforcement authorities;
- Terminate the License Agreement pursuant to Section 11.2 for material AUP violations.

---

## REPORTING VIOLATIONS

To report an AUP violation or abuse, contact: **legal@gatom.io**
