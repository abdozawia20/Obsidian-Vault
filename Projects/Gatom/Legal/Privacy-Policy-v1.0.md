## 1. WHO WE ARE

**Gatom** ("we," "us," "our") is an international group of self-employed individuals operating collaboratively under the shared trading name "Gatom." We provide ERP consulting and implementation services to businesses globally, and operate software products including the Asset Rental platform.

> *Gatom is not a registered legal entity. It is an unincorporated collaboration of independent freelancers. Individual members operate from different locations internationally.*

For data protection purposes, Gatom acts as:
- **Data Controller** — for data collected from visitors to our website and prospects
- **Data Processor** — for Client Data processed on behalf of Licensees under our Software License Agreement and Data Processing Agreement

This Privacy Policy applies to our website, web applications, and all services offered by Gatom.

## 8. COOKIES POLICY

### What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. We use cookies to operate our platform and improve your experience.

### Cookies We Use

| Type | Purpose | Can Opt Out? |
|---|---|---|
| **Essential** | Login sessions, security tokens, preference settings | No — required for platform operation |
| **Analytics** | Understanding how users navigate the platform (privacy-preserving, no personal tracking) | Yes — opt out in cookie settings |
| **Marketing** | Tracking effectiveness of campaigns | Yes — opt out in cookie settings or by not consenting |

### Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through:
- Our cookie settings panel (available on first visit and via footer link)
- Your browser settings
- Opting out of analytics via our cookie preference center

Withdrawing consent for non-essential cookies does not affect platform functionality.
